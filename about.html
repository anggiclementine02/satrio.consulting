Our Story

Born from Experience Across Borders and Industries

Most recruiters talk about talent. We've been the talent.
Satrio Consulting was built on a foundation of lived experience — years spent working across Indonesia, East Asia, and Europe, in roles spanning game development, e-commerce, and renewable energy.

Our founder's computer science background and cross-industry career means we don't just match résumés to job descriptions. We understand the technical depth, the business context, and what it truly takes to excel in a role.

That perspective is our edge — and the market has taken notice. Multinational companies across APAC now rely on us for their most important talent decisions, trusting us to deliver candidates who don't just fill a seat but move the business forward.

We partner with ambitious companies to deliver the right people, with precision, proven expertise, and zero guesswork.
