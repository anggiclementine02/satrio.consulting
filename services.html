Recruitment Process
Outsourcing (RPO)

A flexible, subscription-based model designed to fill 5–10 junior level positions at a more affordable price. No placement fees or hidden charges. Ideal when you need to scale hiring quickly without the cost of traditional search.

We manage sourcing, screening, and candidate coordination — delivering candidates within the month, with daily progress reports and regular feedback calls with your dedicated Recruitment Advisor.

  • Fill 5–10 positions per 1-month engagement
  • No placement fee — flat subscription model
  • Filled in one month, no long-term contract
  • Daily progress reports
  • Scheduled feedback calls with Recruitment Advisor
  • Tailor-made strategy aligned to your hiring needs

03

Employer of Record (EOR) / BPO

Hire and manage employees in Indonesia without setting up a local entity. Through our Employer of Record (EOR) service, Satrio Consulting legally employs talent on your behalf while you retain full control over their daily work and performance.

We handle all local employment responsibilities — including payroll, tax, contracts, and compliance with Indonesian labor regulations — allowing you to expand your team in Indonesia quickly and with minimal administrative burden.

  • Hire talent in Indonesia without establishing a local company
  • Full compliance with Indonesian labor laws, BPJS, and PPh 21 tax regulations
  • End-to-end payroll management and employee administration
  • Employment contracts, documentation, and legal compliance handled
  • Religious Holiday Allowance (THR) managed according to local regulations
  • Transparent monthly structure: salary + statutory benefits + service fee
